How to Get a Hulu Free Trial?

  1. Visit the Hulu website or download the Hulu app on your device.
  2. Click on the "Hulu Free Trial" or similar option prominently displayed on the homepage.
  3. Create a Hulu account by providing your email address, password, and other required information.
  4. Choose the subscription plan that offers a free trial (usually indicated with the trial duration).
  5. If prompted, enter your payment information. You won't be charged during the trial period.
  6. Complete the sign-up process and start exploring Hulu's content library immediately.
  7. Enjoy unlimited streaming of TV shows, movies, and original series during the trial period.
  8. Remember to cancel your subscription before the trial ends if you decide not to continue. You can usually do this through your account settings.
